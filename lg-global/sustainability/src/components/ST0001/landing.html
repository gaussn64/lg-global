When we create a better life for all.

A better life for all

We believe 'Life's Good', but to enjoy it, we must look after it.
That's why we're calling on everyone to create a better life for all.
We're redesigning processes to cut carbon, rethinking product lifecycles
to reduce waste, introducing new tech and products to foster sustainability,
and building safe, inclusive, and accessible environments for everyone to enjoy life.
These are our commitments and our way of creating a better life for all.

We're committed to making a better impact.

  • Carbon neutrality For a long-lasting planet
  • Circularity For a sustainable product lifecycle
  • Clean technology For a better natural environment
  • Delightful workplace For a healthy and safe environment
  • Diversity & Inclusion For a fair and equal society
  • Design for all For an accessible environment to everyone

How to make progress on our commitments

For the planet

Sustainability
in everyday living

Sunday dinners. Movie nights. Fresh sheets. A morning breeze. A home life that restores you. Our shared sustainability journey means we can cherish these precious moments for generations to come. When we work together to create a more sustainable way of life, we all reap the rewards.

Learn more

Carbon neutrality

Major actions.
Minor carbons.

Nature always had our attention. But its message is urgent:
to reduce carbon emissions. We are committed to an aggressive schedule toward carbon neutrality and using 100% renewable energy. The sooner we minimize our impact, there will be fewer "natural" hazards, and more nature to appreciate.

Learn more

Circularity

Greener with
every step

Imagine a world with no waste. That's what we strive for with our sustainable cycle. By recycling all we can to turn old materials and appliances into new products, we're doing our part to reduce waste and create a more sustainable future for our planet.

Learn more

Clean technology

Leading the way
towards a cleaner
tomorrow

We're developing innovative technologies that reduce carbon emissions, conserve resources and make living more sustainable. Our unwavering commitment propels us towards a cleaner and greener future.

Learn more

For people

From people,
for people

We are committed to upholding human rights, fostering diversity, and creating a supportive work environment.
We aim to create a sense of belonging, enabling individuals to reach their full potential. With a focus on exceptional experiences, we bring smiles to people's faces through human-centered innovation.

Learn more

our society

We are committed to expanding opportunities for local communities, youth, and individuals with disabilities worldwide. We firmly believe that creating meaningful change requires the collective efforts of all.
